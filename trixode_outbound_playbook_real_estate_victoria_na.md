## 7) Discovery Call Script (15 minutes)

## 7.1 Opening (2 min)

"[FirstName], agenda for this call: quick diagnosis of where your lead flow is leaking, then I will show the fastest path to fix it, and we decide if it is a fit now, later, or not at all. Sound fair?"

## 7.2 Diagnosis questions (5 min)

| Question | Why ask |
|---|---|
| "How many listing and buyer inquiries do you get monthly?" | Establishes baseline volume |
| "How many of those become consult appointments?" | Measures conversion gap |
| "What is your average GCI per side in CAD?" | Enables ROI math |
| "Where is leakage biggest today: traffic, trust, or follow-up speed?" | Finds primary bottleneck |
| "What is your current lead response time?" | High predictor of close rate |
| "What has your team already tried in the last 6 months?" | Avoids repeating failed work |
| "Who else approves this decision?" | Clarifies authority and timeline |

## 7.3 Pivot (3 min): offer + ROI

"Given [PAIN_SUMMARY], the best fit is [OFFER_NAME]. We would prioritize [LEVER_1], [LEVER_2], then [LEVER_3], and tie progress to [KPI_1] and [KPI_2]."

ROI template:

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| Extra leads/month | `[MONTHLY_TRAFFIC] x ([TARGET_CONV]-[CURRENT_CONV])` |
| Extra appointments | `[EXTRA_LEADS] x [APPOINTMENT_RATE]` |
| Extra closed sides | `[EXTRA_APPOINTMENTS] x [CLOSE_RATE]` |
| Revenue lift/month | `[EXTRA_CLOSED_SIDES] x [AVG_GCI_CAD]` |
| Payback months | `[INVESTMENT_CAD] / [REVENUE_LIFT_MONTHLY]` |

## 7.4 Close (3 min)

"Investment for [OFFER_NAME] is [PRICE_CAD]. Timeline is [TIMELINE]. Process is [STEP_1], [STEP_2], [STEP_3]. If this aligns, I can send proposal today and we can start [START_DATE]."

## 7.5 "Need to think about it"

"Totally fair. Usually that means timing, budget, or confidence. Which one is the real blocker? I can send a one-page decision brief tailored for [PARTNER_OR_TEAM] so you can decide quickly with the right context."

## 8) Objection Handling Scripts (Full A-C-R-P-C)

## 8.1 "It is too expensive"

| Step | Script |
|---|---|
| Acknowledge | "Fair point, [FirstName]. You should protect budget." |
| Clarify | "Is this a cash-flow issue, ROI confidence issue, or comparison to another option?" |
| Reframe | "The comparison is [PRICE_CAD] vs cost of missed deals in a market where volume is already tighter." |
| Proof | "In Victoria, one side on a benchmark-priced home can dwarf this investment when conversion improves." |
| Close | "If we use [PAYMENT_OPTION], does this become workable?" |

## 8.2 "Not right now"

| Step | Script |
|---|---|
| Acknowledge | "Makes sense. Timing matters." |
| Clarify | "Is this bandwidth, budget cycle, or priority conflict?" |
| Reframe | "We can phase this: [PHASE_1] now, [PHASE_2] in [MONTH]." |
| Proof | "That phased model has helped overloaded teams still capture quick wins." |
| Close | "Would a phased start on [DATE] be better than waiting?" |

## 8.3 "How do I know this works?"

| Step | Script |
|---|---|
| Acknowledge | "Great question." |
| Clarify | "What would make this feel safe: milestones, references, or guarantee terms?" |
| Reframe | "You should not rely on promises. You should rely on tracked KPIs and milestones." |
| Proof | "We define [KPI_1], [KPI_2], [KPI_3] up front and review at each milestone." |
| Close | "If we include that in writing, are you comfortable moving ahead?" |

## 8.4 "I do not think I need this"

| Step | Script |
|---|---|
| Acknowledge | "Fair - if your system is performing, you should protect it." |
| Clarify | "What is your current conversion from web lead to consult to signed client?" |

| Proof | "Most teams feel 'fine' until they measure response lag and consult conversion losses." |
| Close | "Open to a diagnostic first so we verify if a leak exists?" |

## 8.5 "Let me check with my partner"

| Step | Script |

| Proof | "That usually speeds internal decisions because it removes ambiguity." |
| Close | "Should I send that today and hold [FOLLOWUP_DATE] for a 3-way call?" |

## 8.6 "How are you different?"

|---|---|
